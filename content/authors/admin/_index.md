## About Me

I am a student of the Integrated M.Sc. Physics program at the UM-DAE Centre for Excellence in Basic Sciences, Mumbai. My research experience includes working at TIFR, Mumbai on mapping plasma critical density surfaces using wavefront measurements and ultrafast diagnostics. I also have experience in simulating plasma heating using Particle-in-Cell (PIC) codes and have conducted literature reviews on the applications of statistical physics in biology. I am passionate about understanding the complex dynamics of plasmas and exploring the frontiers of intense laser sciences.
